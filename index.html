Crystal Collector

Each crystal has a hidden value that will only be revealed when clicked. With each click, you will add the crystal's value to your total score. You will win the game if your total score matches the magic number, and you will lose if your total score exceeds it. In the words of the immortal Han Solo, "Good luck, you're gonna need it!"
